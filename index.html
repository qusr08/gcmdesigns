Hi! My name is Gabriela Colon-Melendez and I am a second year Industrial Design student at the Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University in the College of Architecture and Urban Studies. I am extremely grateful to have the opportunity to study in such a successful and challenging program. I have been passionate about design, art, and innovation for as long as I can remember but I began fostering that appreciation much more seriously in high school while on my school's robotics team as a mechanical subteam member my 1st year and a design/CAD subteam member the remaining 3.

I have already grown so much being a part of VTID and I am very eager to continue learning more about sustainable and ergonomic design as well as expanding my 3D modeling/rendering knowledge to have more variety in my software catalog.
