DOS (the number two in Spanish) is a single-player puzzle game that was created for the 2022 Game Maker's Toolkit 48 hour game jam. The theme was "Roll of the Dice" and I suggested for us to take the theme literally by creating a game where the player is physically rolling a die. The objective of DOS is to roll the die strategically in order to have its two face facing up! We introduced different tiles that have different mechanics that can move, rotate, or restrict the die which players have to keep in mind in order to reach their objective.

I was responsible for visuals, colors, textures, and contributed to the level design as well. DOS was completed in the 48 hours given and placed in the top 5% out of over 6200 submissions!
