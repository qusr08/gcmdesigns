Roanoke Follies

August 2021

Prompt:

Design and prototype a scaled stand-alone folly to be placed in Downtown Roanoke, Virginia. Then, as a group, work together to create a cohesive and meaningful plan for the multiple structures and their sites. How will the people of Downtown interact, use, or view these structures? What do the follies suggest or provoke? How do they communicate with the space around them?
