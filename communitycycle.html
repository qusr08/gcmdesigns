Community Cycle

January 2022

Prompt:

For the VTID 2022 Design Competition, groups will propose a design intervention that can help improve or replace one of the following scenarios:

  • Recycling streams from the home to recycle center: For this user group, consider a multi-person family that is struggling to keep up with the recyclable items they bring into their home.
  • Single use items in local businesses: For this user group, consider a local restaurant, food store, or relevant establishment that wants to reduce or eliminate waste from their business model.
  • Community consumption of single use items: For this user group, consider how an individual or group can conserve resources or achieve zero waste policies and procedures in their personal or shared space.
