Flatware

May 2022

Prompt:

Using all of the skills you've learned this semester, model a set of flatware.This must include at least 5 utensils: 1 knife, 1 fork and 1 spoon + 2 additional utensils (5 differently shaped and used utensils/implements total). The 2 additional utensils should be either secondary utensils, serving implements or similarly related specialty use pieces.

All pieces must be visually and form-wise a related family of utensils.

You are expected to research and use your creative design skills to produce a well-thought-out set. Take this design opportunity seriously and make them believable, practical and desirable. Choose forms and material(s) wisely.
